ABOUT ME

Hello there! I’m Dinuki, a UX and UI designer currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU. In particular, I deeply enjoy exploring the nuances and complexities of the human experience. As I continue to learn and develop my methods as a designer, I look forward to applying my perspective and skillsets on the road ahead.

INSPIRATIONS

Graphically, I admire clean lines, geometric shapes, and organic patterns but find equal fascination in well-curated clutters and clusters. Since childhood, I’ve been in love with storytelling – exploration and growth – and how to best implement our experiences into our work.
