Hello there! I’m Dinuki, a UX and UI designer currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU. In particular, I deeply enjoy exploring the nuances and complexities of the human experience. As I continue to learn and develop my methods as a designer, I look forward to applying my perspective and skillsets on the road ahead.

As our term project for IAT 343: Animation, my team and I modelled, animated, and edited together Coco, a short film about a curious coconut who leaves his home tree to explore the mysterious island around him. The project was completed using AutoDesk MAYA, Adobe Premiere, and Adobe AfterEffects.

For IAT 267, my team and I were tasking with creating an interactive system using sensors, an Arduino microcontroller and Processing for programming.
