DINUKI ADIKARAM

Hello there! I’m Dinuki, a user experience and media designer currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU. Both as a designer and as a ‘perpetual work-in-progress,’ I always strive for exploration and growth. Graphically, I deeply admire clean lines, geometric shapes, and organic patterns but find equal fascination in well-curated clutters and clusters. Since childhood, I’ve taken an interest in storytelling through art which I currently work to implement into my design work. My goal in my work is to thoroughly understand my users in order to design solutions that are intuitive and shine in contexts.
